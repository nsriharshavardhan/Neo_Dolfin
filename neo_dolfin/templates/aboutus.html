    Acknowledgment

 

The developers at DolFin would like to acknowledge the research undertaken by the Melbourne Institute in collaboration with the Commonwealth Bank of Australia (CBA) into the field of Financial Wellbeing. Their partnership has been pivotal in shaping the financial wellbeing models integrated into this application.

The reported and observed financial wellbeing scales developed by the Melbourne Institute-CBA partnership serves as a cornerstone for DolFin's mission to empower users with actionable insights into their financial health. By implementing both the reported and observed wellbeing scales developed by this partnership, DolFin ensures that users receive a holistic understanding of their financial position, thus fostering informed decision-making and improved financial outcomes.
